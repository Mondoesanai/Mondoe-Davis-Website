Curriculum

3 Stages. 8 Modules. One Transformation.

Every module is built around the Clarity → Communication → Leadership framework. By the end, you won't just know more — you'll be more.

01
[Clarity] Identity — Know Who You Are

Strip away the labels the world gave you. Discover the man God designed from the beginning.

02
[Clarity] Purpose — Find Your God-Given Why

Stop drifting. Identify the specific calling and mission that drives every decision you make.

03
[Communication] Voice — Unlock Your Communication

Learn to speak with clarity, conviction, and power. Say what you mean and be heard the first time.

04
[Communication] Confidence — Own the Room

Build the internal confidence that changes how you walk, talk, and show up in every environment.

05
[Leadership] Leadership — Lead at Home First

The framework for leading your family with strength, presence, and intentionality every day.

06
[Leadership] Discipline — Build the System

Create habits, routines, and rhythms that compound into the life you're meant to live. Discipline creates freedom.

07
Faith — Anchor Everything

Ground your identity, leadership, and communication in your faith. This is the foundation everything else is built on.

08
Legacy — Build Something That Lasts

Define the legacy you're building. The man who completes this module leaves a mark on every generation after him.
